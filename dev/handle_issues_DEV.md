# How does Issues work
## Inspiration
Imagine you are alone on a friday night at a 21:43 Pm and you realize how a good cahange could improve our project. BANG! You create a *issue*

## How create a new Issue
In the menu up on the left you got a <code>Issue</code> botton (between <code>Code</code> and <code>Pull requests</code>)<br>
Then <code>New issue</code> <br>
Add a title and some description <br>
On the left you got a little menu where you can find the item: *labels*, click on that and select *discuss* <br>
In the and click on <code>Create</code> <br>
